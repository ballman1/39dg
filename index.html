About 39DG

39DG is the parent company of a portfolio of innovative eyewear brands dedicated to making advanced vision solutions accessible to everyone. Founded in 2010, we've grown from a single online store to a family of specialized brands serving diverse vision needs.

Our mission is to combine optical excellence with technological innovation while maintaining affordable pricing that democratizes access to quality vision care.

Our Vision

We believe everyone deserves clear, comfortable vision without financial barriers. By vertically integrating our manufacturing and distribution, we're able to offer premium products at revolutionary prices.

Each brand in our portfolio addresses specific vision challenges with scientifically-backed solutions developed by our optical engineering team.
